Our Mission

Owing to the fact that a good basic education is a foundation of excellence in academia as well as life, we strive to provide less privileged children with all the necessary materials required for schooling at the basic level. Our aim is to get as many children as possible to value and have a good basic education.

Education is a human right with immense power to transform. On its foundation rest the cornerstones of freedom, democracy, and sustainable human development.
